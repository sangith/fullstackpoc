Welcome to the online submission and editorial system for Trends in Cancer.

Unsolicited submissions. Articles for Trends in Cancer are generally commissioned by the Editor, but ideas for articles are welcome. Due to differences in our editorial process, we are unable to receive papers transferred directly from other journals. For further information or to view journal content please go to: http://www.cell.com/trends/trecan.

Having trouble? Please call 1-617-397-2800. You can also send an email to trendscancer@cell.com.

First-time users: Please click on the "REGISTER" button to the left on this page and enter the requested information. Upon successful registration, you will be sent an e-mail with instructions to verify your registration. NOTE: If you received an e-mail from us with an assigned user ID and password, DO NOT REGISTER AGAIN. Simply use that information to login. Usernames and passwords may be changed after registration (see instructions below).
Repeat users: Please click the "LOG IN" button on the left and proceed as appropriate.
Authors: Please click the "LOG IN" button on the left and login to the system as "Author." You may then submit your manuscript and track its progress through the system.
Reviewers: Please click the "LOG IN" button on the left and login to the system as "Reviewer." You may then view and/or download manuscripts assigned to you for review or submit your comments to the editor and the authors.
To change your username and/or password: Once you are registered, you may change your contact information, username and/or password at any time. Simply log in to the system and click on "Update My Information" in the navigation bar at the top of the page.
